# Landing Page Design
**Purpose:** Design high-conversion landing pages — the page architecture, visual hierarchy, and CTA strategy that turns visitors into leads.

## The Practice
Design is where the agency's craft becomes visible. The quality of design execution is the quality of the agency's reputation.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Landing Page Design must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

# Motion Design for Agencies
**Purpose:** Design motion and animation for client projects — micro-interactions, page transitions, and the motion language that adds polish.

## The Practice
Design is where the agency's craft becomes visible. The quality of design execution is the quality of the agency's reputation.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Motion Design for Agencies must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

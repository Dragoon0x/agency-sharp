# Social Media Design
**Purpose:** Design social media content systems — templates, brand consistency, platform optimization, and the visual language that works in feeds.

## The Practice
Design is where the agency's craft becomes visible. The quality of design execution is the quality of the agency's reputation.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Social Media Design must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

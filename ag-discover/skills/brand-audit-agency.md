# Brand Audit
**Purpose:** Audit brand consistency across touchpoints — identifying where the brand is strong, where it's diluted, and where it's missing.

## The Practice
Discovery is the phase that most agencies skip and most projects suffer for. Understanding the problem before solving it is the highest-leverage investment in any engagement.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Brand Audit must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

# Post-Launch Monitoring
**Purpose:** Monitor the launch — error rates, performance metrics, user behavior, and the real-time indicators that something needs immediate attention.

## The Practice
Launch is where reputations are made or broken. A flawed launch undermines months of work. A clean launch builds trust that lasts.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Post-Launch Monitoring must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

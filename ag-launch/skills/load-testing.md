# Load Testing
**Purpose:** Test performance under expected traffic load — ensuring the infrastructure handles launch-day traffic without degradation.

## The Practice
Launch is where reputations are made or broken. A flawed launch undermines months of work. A clean launch builds trust that lasts.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Load Testing must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

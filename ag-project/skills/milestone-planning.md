# Milestone Planning
**Purpose:** Apply milestone planning with the depth and rigor that agency delivery demands — where client expectations, fixed budgets, and quality standards leave no room for generic approaches.

## The Practice
Agency project management — planning, tracking, communication, risk management, and the coordination that keeps multi-project, multi-client work on track

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Milestone Planning must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

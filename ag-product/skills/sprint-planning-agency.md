# Sprint Planning Agency
**Purpose:** Apply sprint planning agency with the depth and rigor that agency delivery demands — where client expectations, fixed budgets, and quality standards leave no room for generic approaches.

## The Practice
The product management playbook — strategy, discovery, execution, metrics, and the product thinking that ensures agency-built products solve real problems

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Sprint Planning Agency must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

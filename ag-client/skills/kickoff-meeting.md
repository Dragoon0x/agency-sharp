# Project Kickoff Meeting
**Purpose:** Run kickoff meetings that align everyone and set the project on the right trajectory from day one.

## The Practice
Clients are the lifeblood. The relationship between agency and client determines everything — project quality, profitability, referrals, and whether the work is sustainable.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Project Kickoff Meeting must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

# Client Presentation Skills
**Purpose:** Present work to clients in a way that builds confidence and directs feedback productively.

## The Practice
Clients are the lifeblood. The relationship between agency and client determines everything — project quality, profitability, referrals, and whether the work is sustainable.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Client Presentation Skills must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

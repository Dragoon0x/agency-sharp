## The Philosophy

A great agency is not a collection of talented people. It's a system.

The system determines whether projects are profitable or subsidized. Whether clients become long-term partners or one-time transactions. Whether the team scales gracefully or collapses under its own weight. Whether quality holds at 50 projects or degrades after 5.

Most agency knowledge lives in the founder's head — pricing instincts, client management habits, quality standards that exist as feelings rather than processes. That works at 3 people. It breaks at 10. It's catastrophic at 30.

**agency-sharp encodes the entire system into three layers:**

1. **Agency Operations** — The business: positioning, client management, scoping, pricing, finance, scaling
2. **Production Pipeline** — The delivery: discovery → design → build → test → launch → grow
3. **Role Playbooks** — The craft depth: creative, product, engineering, and project management

The hierarchy is:
1. **Ops** — Is the agency positioned, priced, and structured to be sustainable?
2. **Client** — Are client relationships healthy, profitable, and growing?
3. **Scope** — Is every project scoped and priced to protect margin?
4. **Discover** — Does every project start with understanding before execution?
5. **Design** — Does design execution match the strategy and meet the quality bar?
6. **Build** — Does engineering deliver maintainable, performant, accessible code?
7. **Launch** — Does every launch go smoothly with no post-launch surprises?
8. **Grow** — Does the work produce measurable results after launch?
9. **Handoff** — Do transitions between phases preserve quality and context?
10. **Orchestrate** — Can multiple roles coordinate on complex deliverables?
11. **Creative** — Does the creative work stand out in a market of sameness?
12. **Product** — Does the product thinking ensure the right thing gets built?
13. **Engineering** — Does the technical execution set a professional standard?
14. **Project** — Does the coordination keep everything on track?
15. **Scale** — Can the agency grow without breaking what made it good?

Get the system right and quality compounds. Skip a layer and effort accumulates without results.

# Agency Legal Basics
**Purpose:** Handle the legal fundamentals — MSAs, SOWs, IP ownership, liability, and the contract structures that protect the agency.

## The Practice
The business behind the business. Positioning, service design, team structure, financial ops, and the operational infrastructure that makes an agency sustainable.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Agency Legal Basics must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

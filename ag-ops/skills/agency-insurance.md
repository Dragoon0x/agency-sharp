# Agency Insurance
**Purpose:** Understand the insurance an agency needs — E&O, general liability, cyber, and the coverage that prevents catastrophic exposure.

## The Practice
The business behind the business. Positioning, service design, team structure, financial ops, and the operational infrastructure that makes an agency sustainable.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Agency Insurance must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

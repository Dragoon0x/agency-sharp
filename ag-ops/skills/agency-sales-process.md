# Agency Sales Process
**Purpose:** Convert prospects into clients — the agency sales process from lead to signed contract, including qualifying, scoping, proposing, and closing.

## The Practice
The business behind the business. Positioning, service design, team structure, financial ops, and the operational infrastructure that makes an agency sustainable.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Agency Sales Process must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

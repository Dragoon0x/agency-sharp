# Agency Automation Strategy
**Purpose:** Automate agency operations — invoicing, reporting, project setup, and the repetitive tasks that consume team time.

## The Practice
Scaling an agency means rebuilding the machine while it's running. Every growth stage breaks the systems that got you there.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Agency Automation Strategy must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

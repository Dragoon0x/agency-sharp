# Revenue Model Evolution
**Purpose:** Evolve the revenue model — from project-based to retainer-based to productized, and the specific transitions that increase revenue predictability.

## The Practice
Scaling an agency means rebuilding the machine while it's running. Every growth stage breaks the systems that got you there.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Revenue Model Evolution must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

# Paid Advertising Optimization
**Purpose:** Optimize paid campaigns — Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads — improving ROAS through targeting, creative, and landing page optimization.

## The Practice
Launch is the beginning, not the end. Growth is where launched products become performing products — through data, experimentation, and continuous improvement.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Paid Advertising Optimization must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

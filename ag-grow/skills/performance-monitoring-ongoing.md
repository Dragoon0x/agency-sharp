# Ongoing Performance Monitoring
**Purpose:** Monitor production performance continuously — uptime, speed, error rates, and the alerts that catch degradation before users report it.

## The Practice
Launch is the beginning, not the end. Growth is where launched products become performing products — through data, experimentation, and continuous improvement.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Ongoing Performance Monitoring must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

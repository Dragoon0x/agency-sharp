# Content Performance Optimization
**Purpose:** Optimize content for engagement and conversion — measuring what content works, why, and how to produce more of it.

## The Practice
Launch is the beginning, not the end. Growth is where launched products become performing products — through data, experimentation, and continuous improvement.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Content Performance Optimization must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

**Purpose:** Improve organic search performance — technical SEO, content optimization, link strategy, and the specific improvements that move rankings.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. SEO Optimization must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use

# Deployment & DevOps for Agencies
**Purpose:** Deploy and host client projects — Vercel, Netlify, AWS, and the infrastructure decisions that balance performance, cost, and maintainability.

## The Practice
Engineering is where designs become real. Agency engineering has unique constraints — client codebases, tight timelines, and the need to hand off maintainable work.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Deployment & DevOps for Agencies must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

# Testing for Agency Projects
**Purpose:** Test agency deliverables — the minimum viable testing strategy that catches critical issues without blowing the budget.

## The Practice
Engineering is where designs become real. Agency engineering has unique constraints — client codebases, tight timelines, and the need to hand off maintainable work.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. Testing for Agency Projects must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

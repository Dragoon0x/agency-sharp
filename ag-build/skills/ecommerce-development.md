# E-Commerce Development
**Purpose:** Build e-commerce experiences — Shopify, WooCommerce, or custom — with the conversion optimization and payment integration expertise that agency clients expect.

## The Practice
Engineering is where designs become real. Agency engineering has unique constraints — client codebases, tight timelines, and the need to hand off maintainable work.

## The Agency Context
Agency work has constraints that product teams don't — multiple clients, parallel projects, fixed budgets, external deadlines, and the constant tension between quality and speed. E-Commerce Development must account for these constraints to be practically useful.

## When to use
When this is the active constraint in client delivery or agency operations.

# Progressive Enhancement Engineering
**Purpose:** Apply progressive enhancement engineering in the agency context — where multiple clients, parallel projects, and fixed budgets make every operational decision higher-stakes than in-house work.

## The Agency Difference
The engineering craft playbook — architecture, frontend, backend, DevOps, security, and the technical depth that makes agency engineering stand out For progressive enhancement engineering specifically, the key is building repeatable systems that work across clients without reinventing them for each engagement.

## When to use
When this operational or delivery element is the bottleneck. When scaling past what ad-hoc execution can handle.
